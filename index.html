About Us




Serving the Greater Seattle area, Cheap Towing is a licensed, bonded, and insured company with over a years of experience. We are your personal roadside assistance network, available anytime, anywhere. We offer a variety of services to help you get back on the road sooner. We have our own fleet of drivers, and also partner with some of the best service providers in the area. Our drivers and affiliated companies who partner with us represent our company and are therefore carefully vetted. Just make a call or text everything is digitalized and easy way to get Quick Response and help will be on the way!
